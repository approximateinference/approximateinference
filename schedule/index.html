This year AABI will be a virtual event consisting of two days online seminars held in February 1st-2nd, 2022. Each seminar will be broadcast via Zoom and in the meantime live-streamed on AABI Youtube channel. The Zoom registration is free but will be limited. Note that each seminar needs to be registered individually.
